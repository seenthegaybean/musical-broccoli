PLEASE READ: POLICY

Rates and Deposit: Babysitting services are charged at $10 per hour, with a non-refundable deposit of $15 required at the time of booking. This deposit can be used to cover the cost of the first hour of babysitting.
‍ Booking Procedure: Book your session online by clicking the black button at the top of the website that says "SCHEDULE A SESSION."
‍ Emergency Procedures: For minor cuts or scrapes, first aid will be administered, and if necessary, emergency services will be contacted.
‍ Behavior Expectations: Children are expected not to harm the babysitter, siblings, pets, or property. If necessary, approved disciplinary measures such as time-outs will be implemented.
‍ Activities: Activities will be tailored to the child's preferences and interests.
‍ Screen Time Policy: Screen time is available with the purchase of the $8 Entertainment package. Damage to the provided tablet will incur a fee of $2000, assessed by Geek Squad at Best Buy. Audio recordings of the damage assessment are available upon request.
‍ Feeding and Meal Policies: Parents may leave a food budget or provide food for their child. Food budget selection must be made at the time of booking.
‍ Transportation: Babysitting services will be provided at the client's home.
‍ Privacy and Confidentiality: Your privacy will always be respected.
‍ Backup Plan: If the babysitter is unable to fulfill a scheduled session due to illness, a full refund will be provided.
‍ Booking Requirements: Bookings must be made at least one week in advance. Emergency bookings require a $16 deposit and cash-only food budgets for pizza due to limitations with the payment provider.
‍ Payment: The hourly rate will be charged at $10, minus the $10 deposit. The deposit must be paid at least one week before booking a session.
Payment Methods: Invoice (Card or Bank): Payments can be made securely via credit card or bank transfer through our invoicing system. Invoices will be sent electronically and via mail after to the scheduled babysitting session.
‍ Cash at Arrival of House: Cash payments are accepted upon arrival at your home for the babysitting session. Please have the exact amount ready to expedite the payment process.
‍ Cash App: We also accept payments through Cash App for added flexibility. Our Cash App username will be provided upon request.
